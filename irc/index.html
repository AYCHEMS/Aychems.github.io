Blackcoin IRC Guidelines

The #blackcoin IRC channel is a public chat room hosted on Freenode. Anyone who wishes to know more about Blackcoin is welcome.

Channel Rules

The following activities are unacceptable:

  1. Illegal activities or discussions that are against Freenode's policies.
  2. Scamming or impersonation
  3. Spamming or flooding
  4. Operating malicious scripts.
  5. Posting malicious or referral links
  6. Hate speech or personal attacks
  7. Flaming or rallying people against other coins
  8. Trolling
  9. Begging

The interpretation of rules is at the channel operator's discretion.

  • !deposit - View your deposit address
  • !tip <user> <amount> - Tip BLK to another user

BearBot

Owner: SyllaBear
General commands:
  • !help - Display available commands
  • !market - Check the current price of BLK across several exchanges
  • !info - Check current block height and other information
  • !convert - Convert different fiat and cryptocurrencies.
    example: !convert 1 btc blk
  • !addr - Check the balance of BLK or BTC address.
    example: !addr BEAR1yoKwmkyyUjYRvh4i6xRGujeFWvLVT
Rain related commands:

BearBot also rains active users with BLK. Use !tip BearBot <amount> to rain.

  • !active - Show number of active users
  • !activelist - List nicks of active users
